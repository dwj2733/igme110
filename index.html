Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 is one of the most anticipated games of the next year, but the design and story are not the only impact it will have on the future of gaming.

CD Projekt Red is breaking new ground in the game development industry with Cyberpunk 2077 by focusing on reducing crunch, promoting inclusivity, and having a focus on the quality of the game.



The game development industry has many issues that have been consistent over the last few decades. CD Projekt Red, the company behind the Witcher series and the upcoming game Cyberpunk 2077, has the potential to change the way game companies design their games and treat their employees. While CD Projekt Red still suffers from some of the same downfalls that most companies do, such as a need for excessive amounts of crunch time, pressure to meet deadlines, and pressure from producers to make a profit off the game, they have made major steps to be better than other companies in the industry and are widely respected for that.
